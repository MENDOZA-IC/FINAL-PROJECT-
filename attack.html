Attack Items

Blade of Despair

Massive physical attack boost item designed for finishing enemies. Grants very high damage scaling and increases damage when enemies are low HP. Best for burst assassins and late game marksman.

Berserker Fury

Critical strike item that increases crit damage and chance. Enhances sustained DPS during late game fights. Best paired with other crit based equipment.

Endless Battle

Hybrid item giving attack, cooldown reduction, and true damage after skills. Ideal for fighters relying on skill combos.

Haas Claws

Provides strong lifesteal allowing heroes to recover HP while attacking. Used by sustain marksman or melee fighters.

Malefic Roar

Physical penetration item effective against tanks. Increases damage depending on enemy defense.

Demon Hunter Sword

Deals damage based on enemy HP. Strong against tank compositions.

Windtalker

Attack speed item that triggers lightning chain attacks. Improves wave clearing and farming speed.

Rose Gold Meteor

Provides shield when HP is low. Helps damage dealers survive burst.

Dagger

Basic early game physical item. Used mainly as component for advanced weapons.

Great Dragon Spear

Provides physical attack and critical chance. Increases movement speed after using ultimate skills. Ideal for heroes that rely on ultimate burst combos.

Hunter Strike

Grants physical penetration and cooldown reduction. Provides movement speed boost after consecutive hits. Very useful for assassins and aggressive fighters.

Corrosion Scythe

Increases attack speed and applies slow effect on enemies. Best for sustained damage marksman.

Golden Staff

Converts critical chance into attack speed. Allows faster stacking effects for basic attack heroes.

War Axe

Stacking damage item that increases physical attack during combat. Best used by fighters during extended battles.

Legion Sword

Mid-tier physical attack item that increases raw damage. Often used as component for advanced weapons.

Javelin

Provides attack speed and chance to deal bonus damage. Useful early build item for marksman.

Rogue Meteor

Grants physical attack and defensive shield when HP becomes low. Intermediate sustain item for damage dealers.

Iron Hunting Bow

Increases damage dealt to jungle monsters. Helpful early jungle farming tool.
