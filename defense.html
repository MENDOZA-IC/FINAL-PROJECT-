Defense Items

Athena Shield

Protects against burst magic damage and grants shield after taking magic hits.

Antique Cuirass

Reduces enemy physical damage when attacked. Strong against fighters and assassins.

Immortality

Revives hero after death and grants shield. Extremely valuable late game survival tool.

Dominance Ice

Reduces attack speed and healing effects of enemies nearby. Useful against lifesteal and regen heroes.

Blade Armor

Reflects physical damage back to attackers. Very effective against high attack speed marksman.

Oracle

Enhances shield and regeneration effects. Best for sustain tanks and supports.

Radiant Armor

Reduces continuous magic damage over time. Strong against damage-over-time mages.

Twilight Armor

Reduces burst damage from strong physical attacks. Good counter against critical strike enemies.

Cursed Helmet

Deals burn damage to nearby enemies. Improves jungle and wave clearing for tanks.

Steel Legplates

Grants physical defense and reduces damage from repeated attacks. Good early defense item.

Silence Robe

Provides magic resistance and reduces magic damage taken.

Vitality Crystal

Provides additional maximum HP. Basic tank build component.

Molten Essence

Deals continuous burn damage to nearby enemies. Used as component for burning defense items.
