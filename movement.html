Movement Boots

Warrior Boots

Provides physical defense and movement speed.

Tough Boots

Reduces crowd control duration and magic defense.

Magic Shoes

Reduces skill cooldown.

Arcane Boots

Provides magic penetration.

Rapid Boots

Provides highest movement speed but reduces during combat.

Demon Shoes

Restores mana after killing enemies or assisting. Helps heroes with high mana consumption.

Swift Boots

Provides high attack speed bonus. Used by basic attack marksman.
