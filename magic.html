Magic Items

Lightning Truncheon

Burst magic item that triggers chain lightning. Effective for heroes with large mana pool.

Holy Crystal

Provides huge scaling magic power. Increases overall spell damage significantly.

Genius Wand

Reduces enemy magic defense when hitting with skills. Strong against squishy enemies.

Clock of Destiny

Scaling item that grows stronger over time. Provides HP, mana, and magic power.

Divine Glaive

Magic penetration item for defeating high defense tanks.

Concentrated Energy

Magic lifesteal item allowing mages to sustain fights.

Blood Wings

Provides massive magic power and additional shield based on magic power. Extremely strong late game mage item.

Ice Queen Wand

Applies slow effects when skills hit enemies. Helps mages control team fights.

Winter Truncheon

Active item that freezes hero temporarily making them invulnerable. Great defensive tool for mages.

Enchanted Talisman

Provides strong mana regeneration and cooldown reduction. Best for skill spam heroes.

Feather of Heaven

Enhances magic damage through basic attacks. Used by hybrid attack mages.

Mystery Codex

Provides cooldown reduction and magic power. Common component for spell focused equipment.

Magic Wand

Basic magic power boosting item used in multiple mage builds.

Exotic Veil

Provides magic power and magic penetration. Helps early game damage output.

Elegant Gem

Grants mana regeneration and healing effect when leveling up. Used in scaling magic equipment.
