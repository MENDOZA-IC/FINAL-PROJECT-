Item Usage Tips

Basic Component Item Tips

Dagger – Best purchased early when building attack speed items quickly.

Knife – Good early game damage booster before upgrading into stronger attack equipment.

Iron Hunting Bow – Very useful for junglers since it increases monster damage and farming speed.

Legion Sword – Used as an early step toward high damage physical items.

Magic Necklace – Helps heroes with high mana consumption survive longer in lane.

Mystery Codex – Helps heroes who rely heavily on skill cooldown reduction.

Elegant Gem – Best for heroes who need sustain through leveling during early game.

Power Crystal – Simple magic damage booster when building mage items.

Vitality Crystal – Good early survival item when facing aggressive enemies.

Leather Jerkin – Helps reduce damage from early physical attackers.

Magic Resist Cloak – Useful when laning against magic damage heroes.

Javelin – Good for heroes who rely on chance-based bonus damage.

Attack Item Tips

Blade of Despair – Best built during late game to finish enemies quickly.

Demon Hunter Sword – Very effective when fighting tank heroes or enemies with high HP.

Endless Battle – Strong on heroes who combine basic attacks and skills repeatedly.

Berserker's Fury – Best for heroes relying on critical strike damage.

Scarlet Phantom – Helps maintain attack speed during long fights.

Windtalker – Useful for wave clearing and fast farming.

Haas Claws – Allows heroes to stay alive during extended fights through lifesteal.

Malefic Roar – Should be used when enemies build high armor defense.

Hunter Strike – Strong for burst damage heroes that require mobility.

Corrosion Scythe – Helps chase enemies and prevent them from escaping.

Golden Staff – Perfect for heroes with attack-based passive abilities.

Magic Item Tips

Clock of Destiny – Build early to gain maximum stat growth over time.

Lightning Truncheon – Effective for burst mages and wave clearing.

Holy Crystal – Best used when maximizing overall magic damage scaling.

Divine Glaive – Necessary when enemies build magic resistance.

Genius Wand – Works best for heroes dealing repeated magic damage.

Ice Queen Wand – Excellent for slowing enemies and controlling team fights.

Concentrated Energy – Allows mages to survive fights by healing through skill damage.

Glowing Wand – Useful against high HP enemies due to burn damage.

Blood Wings – Late game item that increases both power and survivability.

Winter Crown – Used to avoid fatal burst damage during dangerous situations.

Defense Item Tips

Athena's Shield – Use when enemy team has strong burst mages.

Antique Cuirass – Strong against physical skill damage heroes.

Dominance Ice – Effective against enemies with lifesteal or attack speed.

Oracle – Best paired with heroes that use shields or regeneration abilities.

Immortality – Should be purchased during late game team fights.

Guardian Helmet – Useful for heroes who need strong regeneration outside battles.

Radiant Armor – Best against continuous magic damage heroes.

Brute Force Breastplate – Great for fighters who stay in combat for long periods.

Twilight Armor – Helps reduce burst damage from critical attacks.

Boots Tips

Warrior Boots – Recommended when enemy team focuses on physical damage.

Tough Boots – Good against crowd control and magic damage.

Magic Shoes – Helps heroes that rely on frequent skill usage.

Arcane Boots – Used by mages needing penetration.

Swift Boots – Perfect for heroes relying on fast basic attacks.

Rapid Boots – Best for roaming and map rotation.

Jungle Item Tips

Ice Retribution – Helps slow enemies when chasing or securing objectives.

Flame Retribution – Useful for reducing enemy damage output.

Bloody Retribution – Helps sustain jungle heroes during fights.

Roam Item Tips

Encourage – Boosts overall team damage during team fights.

Favor – Provides healing support to teammates.

Dire Hit – Helps eliminate enemies with low HP.

Conceal – Allows teams to engage enemies using surprise attacks.
